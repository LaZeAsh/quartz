    # Anti-Nuclear Movement

    • People feared the possibility of nuclear wars following the Cuban Missile Crisis
      • 1963 - Nuclear Test Ban Treaty
      • 1968 - Nuclear Non-Proliferation Treaty
      • 1972 - SALT (Strategic Arms Limitation Talks)
    • Relations between the US and USSR intensified with the election of Ronald Reagan
      • Referred to the USSR as the “Evil Empire”
      • Two false alarm incidents almost resulted in nuclear war
      • Enacted the Strategic Defense Initiative a program that would create a “missile shield” protecting the US from nuclear attack

    # Soviet Internal Problems

    • The centrally planned (communist) economy was in crisis by the 1980s
      • Workers lacked the incentive to work harder
      • Scarcity of consumer goods led to hoarding and long lines and stores
      • Housing shortage led to the mass production of low quality apartment complexes
    • 1986 - The Chernobyl Disaster
      • Nuclear accident in Ukraine that the Soviet government attempted to cover up, but failed

    # Soviet External Problems

    • Satellite states attempted to break free from Soviet influence
      • 1956 - Hungarian Revolution
      • 1968 - Prague Spring
    • Soviet-Afghan War
      • 1979 - Soviet troops invaded Afghanistan to support the communist government in power
      • Fought against Muslim guerrilla fighter known as mujahideen
      • Withdrew after 10 years of fighting realizing the war was not winnable

    # Fall of the Soviet Union

    • 1985 - Mikhail Gorbachev came to power and introduced reforms
      • Perestroika - Restructing of the Soviet Economy
        • More free market and Less central planning
      • Glasnost - Opening up Soviet society by introducing freedom of speech and more democracy
        • Led to the media reporting on government problems and people protesting
      • Announced that the USSR would not interfere in internal conflicts in Eastern Europe
    • 1989 - Fall of the Berlin Wall
    • 1991 - Warsaw Pact disbaned
    • 1991 - Soviet Union dissolved
