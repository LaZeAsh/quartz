## Notation

det means determinant
| | also means determinant

## Pre-Requisite

The matrix has to be 2 x 2 to be done by hand any other dimensions use calculator to solve

## 2 x 2

det$\Huge{[}$ a b $\Huge{]}$ 
		c d

To find the determinant do ad - bc

### Example

det $\Huge{[}$ 2 1 $\Huge{]}$ 
        -2 3

ad - bc

(2)(3) - (1)(-2)

6 - (-2) = 8
